## Overview

Received a full €12,500 scholarship to join the two-month School of Artificial Intelligence at Pi School.

This intensive program brings together engineers from around the globe to solve real-world machine learning challenges faced by industry.

Over the course of eight weeks, small teams of 2-3 fellows work collaboratively on an industry challenge, creating impactful solutions. Each team is guided by AI coaches from Pi School and supported by external mentors from academia and industry.

## Key Features

- **Developed an automatic contract drafting system using LLMs.
- **Designed and implemented solution to streamline and enhance the contract creation process.
- **Collaborated in a small team of 3 fellows.
- **Successfully accelerated the company’s writing pipeline, and reducing manual effort.

## Technologies Used

- **Next.js**: For fast, server-rendered React applications.
- **Figma**: For creating and managing design assets and prototypes.
- **Styled-Components**: For styling React components with a modular, themable approach.
- **Storybook**: For building an interactive, documented component library.
